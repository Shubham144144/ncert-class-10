Question Paper Pattern 2026-27

Section A – MCQ (20 Questions × 1 Mark = 20 Marks)

Multiple choice questions and assertion-reason questions.

Section B – Very Short Answer (5 Questions × 2 Marks = 10 Marks)

Short answer type questions.

Section C – Short Answer (6 Questions × 3 Marks = 18 Marks)

Each question carries 3 marks.

Section D – Long Answer (4 Questions × 5 Marks = 20 Marks)

Detailed step-by-step answers required.

Section E – Case Study (3 Questions × 4 Marks = 12 Marks)

Real-life based case study questions. Each has sub-parts.

Important Notes for 2026-27

  • Chapter 1 Real Numbers has only 2 exercises (Exercise 1.1 and 1.2). Exercise 1.3 has been removed.
  • All questions in board exam are from NCERT textbook only.
  • Algebra and Geometry carry the highest marks — prepare these first.
  • Case study questions (Section E) are based on real-life situations.
  • Internal assessment of 20 marks is given by school — attend all periodic tests.
