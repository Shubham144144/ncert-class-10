Key Formulas for Cubic Polynomial ax³ + bx² + cx + d with zeroes α, β, γ:

  • α + β + γ = –b/a (sum of zeroes)
  • αβ + βγ + γα = c/a (sum of product of zeroes taken two at a time)
  • αβγ = –d/a (product of all three zeroes)

Question 1

Question:

Verify that the numbers given alongside of the cubic polynomials below are their zeroes. Also verify the relationship between the zeroes and the coefficients in each case:

(i) 2x³ + x² – 5x + 2;   1/2, 1, –2

(ii) x³ – 4x² + 5x – 2;   2, 1, 1

Solution (i): 2x³ + x² – 5x + 2; zeroes = 1/2, 1, –2

Step 1: Verify each zero by substituting in p(x) = 2x³ + x² – 5x + 2

For x = 1/2:

p(1/2) = 2(1/2)³ + (1/2)² – 5(1/2) + 2

= 2(1/8) + 1/4 – 5/2 + 2

= 1/4 + 1/4 – 5/2 + 2

= 2/4 – 10/4 + 8/4 = 0 ✅

For x = 1:

p(1) = 2(1)³ + (1)² – 5(1) + 2 = 2 + 1 – 5 + 2 = 0 ✅

For x = –2:

p(–2) = 2(–2)³ + (–2)² – 5(–2) + 2

= 2(–8) + 4 + 10 + 2 = –16 + 16 = 0 ✅

Step 2: Verify relationship between zeroes and coefficients.

Here a = 2, b = 1, c = –5, d = 2

α = 1/2, β = 1, γ = –2

Sum of zeroes = α + β + γ = 1/2 + 1 + (–2) = 1/2 – 1 = –1/2

–b/a = –1/2 ✅

Sum of products of pairs = αβ + βγ + γα

= (1/2)(1) + (1)(–2) + (–2)(1/2)

= 1/2 – 2 – 1 = 1/2 – 3 = –5/2

c/a = –5/2 ✅

Product of zeroes = αβγ = (1/2)(1)(–2) = –1

–d/a = –2/2 = –1 ✅

Answer: 1/2, 1, –2 are zeroes of 2x³ + x² – 5x + 2. All relationships verified.

Solution (ii): x³ – 4x² + 5x – 2; zeroes = 2, 1, 1

Step 1: Verify each zero.

For x = 2:

p(2) = (2)³ – 4(2)² + 5(2) – 2 = 8 – 16 + 10 – 2 = 0 ✅

For x = 1:

p(1) = (1)³ – 4(1)² + 5(1) – 2 = 1 – 4 + 5 – 2 = 0 ✅

Step 2: Verify relationship.

Here a = 1, b = –4, c = 5, d = –2

α = 2, β = 1, γ = 1

Sum of zeroes = 2 + 1 + 1 = 4

–b/a = –(–4)/1 = 4 ✅

Sum of products of pairs = (2)(1) + (1)(1) + (1)(2) = 2 + 1 + 2 = 5

c/a = 5/1 = 5 ✅

Product of zeroes = 2 × 1 × 1 = 2

–d/a = –(–2)/1 = 2 ✅

Answer: 2, 1, 1 are zeroes of x³ – 4x² + 5x – 2. All relationships verified.

Question 2

Question:

Find a cubic polynomial with the sum, sum of the product of its zeroes taken two at a time, and the product of its zeroes as 2, –7, –14 respectively.

Solution:

Step 1: Let the cubic polynomial be ax³ + bx² + cx + d.

Given: α + β + γ = 2, αβ + βγ + γα = –7, αβγ = –14

Step 2: Using the formulas:

–b/a = 2 → b/a = –2

c/a = –7

–d/a = –14 → d/a = 14

Step 3: Let a = 1. Then:

b = –2, c = –7, d = 14

Answer: Cubic polynomial = x³ – 2x² – 7x + 14

Question 3

Question:

If the zeroes of the polynomial x³ – 3x² + x + 1 are a – b, a, a + b, find a and b.

Solution:

Step 1: Let α = a–b, β = a, γ = a+b

Here the polynomial is x³ – 3x² + x + 1, so p = 1, q = –3, r = 1, s = 1

Step 2: Sum of zeroes = α + β + γ = –q/p

(a–b) + a + (a+b) = –(–3)/1 = 3

3a = 3

a = 1

Step 3: Product of zeroes = αβγ = –s/p

(a–b)(a)(a+b) = –1/1 = –1

a(a²–b²) = –1

1(1–b²) = –1

1 – b² = –1

b² = 2

b = ±√2

Answer: a = 1, b = √2 or b = –√2

Question 4

Question:

If two zeroes of the polynomial x⁴ – 6x³ – 26x² + 138x – 35 are 2 ± √3, find the other zeroes.

Solution:

Step 1: Two zeroes are 2+√3 and 2–√3.

So (x–(2+√3))(x–(2–√3)) is a factor.

= (x–2–√3)(x–2+√3)

= ((x–2)–√3)((x–2)+√3)

= (x–2)² – 3

= x² – 4x + 4 – 3

= x² – 4x + 1 is a factor.

Step 2: Divide x⁴ – 6x³ – 26x² + 138x – 35 by x² – 4x + 1.

Step 3: Factorise x² – 2x – 35

= x² – 7x + 5x – 35

= x(x–7) + 5(x–7)

= (x+5)(x–7)

Zeroes: x = –5 and x = 7

Answer: All four zeroes = 2+√3, 2–√3, –5, 7

Question 5

Question:

If the polynomial x⁴ – 6x³ + 16x² – 25x + 10 is divided by another polynomial x² – 2x + k, the remainder comes out to be x + a. Find k and a.

Solution:

Step 1: Divide x⁴ – 6x³ + 16x² – 25x + 10 by x² – 2x + k.

Step 2: Given remainder = x + a

Compare coefficients:

Coefficient of x: 2k – 9 = 1 → 2k = 10 → k = 5

Constant term: 10 – 8k + k² = a

= 10 – 8(5) + (5)²

= 10 – 40 + 25 = –5

a = –5

Answer: k = 5 and a = –5

Key Points – Exercise 2.4

  • For cubic ax³+bx²+cx+d with zeroes α, β, γ:
  • α+β+γ = –b/a
  • αβ+βγ+γα = c/a
  • αβγ = –d/a
  • To verify zeroes substitute in p(x) — answer must be 0.
  • Use division to find remaining zeroes when some zeroes are given.
