Key Formulas – Chapter 2 Polynomials

For Quadratic Polynomial ax² + bx + c with zeroes α and β:

  • Sum of zeroes: α + β = –b/a
  • Product of zeroes: αβ = c/a
  • Quadratic polynomial = x² – (sum of zeroes)x + (product of zeroes)

Important Points to Remember

  • A polynomial of degree n has at most n zeroes.
  • Zeroes of a polynomial = points where graph crosses the x-axis.
  • A quadratic polynomial can have 0, 1, or 2 zeroes.
  • The graph of a quadratic polynomial is always a parabola.
  • Sum of zeroes = –b/a (note the negative sign).
  • Product of zeroes = c/a.
