About Chapter 1 – Real Numbers

Chapter 1 of Class 10 Maths is Real Numbers. In this chapter you will learn:

  • Euclid's Division Lemma

  • Fundamental Theorem of Arithmetic
  • Rational and Irrational Numbers

Key Formulas – Chapter 1 Real Numbers

  • Euclid's Division Lemma: a = bq + r, where 0 ≤ r < b
  • HCF × LCM = Product of two numbers
  • Fundamental Theorem of Arithmetic: Every composite number can be expressed as a product of primes in a unique way.

Important Points to Remember

  • HCF is always less than or equal to the smaller number.
  • LCM is always greater than or equal to the larger number.
  • If HCF of two numbers is 1, they are called co-prime numbers.
  • √2, √3, √5 are all irrational numbers.
  • A rational number has either terminating or repeating decimal expansion.
